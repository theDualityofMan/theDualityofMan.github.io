    P&P Optica is an optics company that specializes in food safety, particularly in meat production. They develop machines that use visual and hyperspectral cameras to detect foreign contaminants in a production line environment.
These contaminants are identified using artificial intelligence, and the models that are used are developed in-house. Computing is integral to their business model - in AI, machine learning, and the programs used to process the mass amounts of data
for each model. PPO is currently the only company in the food safety industry to use both hyperspectral and visual imaging, giving them a niche spot on the market and the ability to meet customer's needs more effectively.

    For my Summer 2024 co-op work term, I began working at P&P Optica (PPO) as part of an 8 month co-op extending from mid June to the end of the fall school semester in late December.
I am working in their Data Services department as a data technician, and assist with the collection and processing of data. In order to create and train our models, large amounts of data are required, and quality data is integral to a good
machine learning model. We collect large samples of data based on foreign materials that will be found at our customers' facilities, annotate and post-process this data, and finally upload it so our software team can start developing a model.



    My learning goals going into my coop term were as follows:

- Learn how to better approach difficult problems with an open mindset. Develop strategies to divide problems into manageable subunits without getting lost in
the enormity of them.

- Further develop effective teamwork and communication skills, so as to more efficiently collaborate on projects and communicate thought processes.

- Further develop and utilize my skills in Python and/or other programming languages in any available scenario at PPO.



    I was also inspired to develop additional learning goals that pertained more specifically to my tasks at PPO:

- Become involved in all aspects of the data services team, and participate in data collection and post-processing/annotation.

- Travel to one or more customer plants to experience an on-site data collection, and help install or setup one of our machines.

- Learn as much as possible about the hardware and software that go into developing one of our machines.



    I have been successful in completing or partially completing a good number of these goals. I have had multiple times where I have participated in team projects, as my work environment is very team-oriented,
and through these experiences I am now confident in my ability to collaborate and communicate effectively. I have found that I am a very social person in my work environment, but I now would like to improve on communicating
in a more professional manner. I have been faced with problems that I have had to learn how to solve, and have improved significantly in learning how to approach them. I have been given opportunities to explore the PPO codebase, and help program spreadsheets that were integral to maintaining consistent customer data, using JavaScript. As mentioned above, I have worked in both data collection and post-processing scenarios during my time so far, and have
become proficient in the procedures used in both processes. Not all of my goals have been attained, but I hope to further improve upon them in the latter half of my co-op.
